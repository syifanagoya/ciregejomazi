This “magic elixir” (as Gwen Flamberg, Us Weekly’s executive editor of beauty and style, called it) combines essential greens like spinach, cucumber and parsley with the stress-busting adaptogen ashwagandha, mighty antioxidant resveratrol, anti-inflammatory black seed oil, plus creamy MCT oil to “rev up our metabolism,” according to Naomi.

Naomi’s morning meal goes a step further beyond keto, combining many of the nutrients you need to feel great and get glowing! And the recipe is super simple to follow. All ingredients can be scooped up at gnc.com. Then watch the video above and follow the steps!

Naomi Whittel’s Clean & Green Smoothie Recipe:

Ingredients

1 large handful of leafy greens

½ cucumber, cubed

½ avocado

2 tablespoons parsley, chopped

Juice from ½ lemon

1 capsule ashwagandha powder (600mg) *open capsule and add to smoothie

1 capsule resveratrol (500mg) *open capsule and add to smoothie

2 tablespoons black seed oil

2 tablespoons MCT oil

1 scoop vitamin-packed superfood powder

1 cup filtered water

 Process

Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth. Enjoy!
