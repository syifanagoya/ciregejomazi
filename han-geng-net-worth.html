Who is Han Geng?

Han Geng is a Chinese Mandopop singer and actor, widely recognized as the first Chinese member of the South Korean boy band Super Junior.

He embarked on a solo career after leaving the group and has gained international recognition for both his music and acting careers.

When did he start his music career?

He began his music career in 2005 when he debuted as a member of Super Junior. He later became the leader of the subgroup Super Junior-M in 2008 before starting his solo career in 2010.
